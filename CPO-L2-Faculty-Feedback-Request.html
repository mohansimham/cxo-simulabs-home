CPO Level 2: AI-Powered Boardroom Simulations

We are building the next generation of product management simulations and would be grateful to learn from your wide expertise in this area.

What We Have Built

CPO Level 2 is a series of 15 boardroom simulations where students step into a C-suite role at a real global tech company (Airbnb, Spotify, Netflix, Amazon, and others) and navigate competing executive priorities to make a single strategic decision. Each simulation is powered by 4 AI agents with transparent motives, real-time mood tracking, and AI-scored performance evaluation.

Boardroom Mechanics

Unlike the MBA Series (which uses sealed briefs and information asymmetry), CPO Level 2 gives every agent a transparent motive. The student knows what each executive wants before the simulation begins. The challenge is not uncovering hidden data but navigating legitimate competing priorities under pressure.

Transparent Motives
Every agent's agenda is visible upfront. The CPO wants trust features. The CTO wants a platform rebuild. The VP Growth wants expansion. The CEO wants all of it. No hidden information. The drama comes from the tension between legitimate priorities.
Agent Behaviour
Guided: Agents help you think through trade-offs.

Standard: Agents advocate their position. Push back when challenged.

Brutal: Agents are territorial and will pressure you aggressively.
Boardroom Pulse™
Real-time mood tracking across all agents. Data-backed arguments increase engagement. Dismissing an agent's priority drops their mood. The aggregate "Room Temperature" reflects how the boardroom perceives the student's leadership.

Two intensity levers: Agent Behaviour and Time Pressure (Relaxed / Standard 25 min / Boardroom 15 min / Crisis 8 min). No "Starting Information" lever because all data is public by design. No calculations. The entire assessment is on strategic reasoning, framework application, and boardroom engagement.
