The world is full of learning. Courses, certifications, MBAs, YouTube playlists, bootcamps. What's missing is proof that you can apply what you learned. CXO Simulabs™ exists to close that gap.

The Solution

AI-powered boardroom simulations where you take on a role, face real business decisions, and are scored on application — not recall. Every competency is the hero. Every company is just context.

Dragon Fire Bloom™ orchestrates six scored stages. Boardroom Pulse™ tracks real-time emotional dynamics across seven mood states. Iron Clad™ protects all intellectual property server-side.
