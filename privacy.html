Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 March 2026

CXO Simulabs™ ("we," "us," or "our") is an independent educational platform operated by Mohan Simham. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and simulations at cxosimulabs.com and associated GitHub Pages.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our simulations:

  • Name and email address — provided by you during simulation registration, used for OTP verification and certificate generation.
  • Simulation performance data — your scores, answers, and completion status, stored locally on your device.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any other sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email verification — to send a one-time verification code (OTP) via EmailJS before you begin a simulation.
  • Certificate generation — your name appears on your simulation completion record, generated locally in your browser.
  • Waitlist communication — if you sign up for our waitlist, we may contact you about new simulations, tracks, or features.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • EmailJS — to deliver OTP verification emails. Your name and email address are transmitted to EmailJS for this purpose. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — our simulations are hosted on GitHub Pages. See GitHub Privacy Statement.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Your name and email (to pre-fill forms across simulations)
  • Simulation attempt counts and completion status
  • Performance scores

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data.

5. Data Retention

  • Email/OTP data — OTP codes expire after 10 minutes. We do not maintain a database of user emails from simulation registrations.
  • Waitlist emails — retained until you request removal or we no longer need them for communication.
  • Local storage data — persists on your device until you clear it.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data — contact us and we will provide any information we hold.
  • Delete your data — contact us to request deletion of any data we hold. For locally stored data, clear your browser's site data.
  • Opt out of communications — reply to any email with "unsubscribe" or contact us directly.

7. Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. GA4 collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time spent. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. See Google Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

Our simulations are designed for professionals and students aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Our simulations are served over HTTPS.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
