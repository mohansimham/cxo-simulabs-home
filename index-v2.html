Inside Every Simulation

Three Systems. One Experience.

Every simulation is built on the same battle-tested stack. The engine runs the logic. The boardroom reads the room. The platform ties it all together.

DF
Dragon Fire™
The Simulation Engine

Orchestrates the five-phase flow from brief to scoring. Manages AI agent conversations, calculation worksheets, decision capture, panel cross-examination, and rubric-based grading. Every phase is timed, sequenced, and scored.

BP
Boardroom Pulse™
The Mood System

Every AI agent has a real-time mood score (0–100) that shifts based on your questions. Ask the right things and the room opens up. Push too hard and agents shut down, deflect, or turn hostile. Seven mood states from Hostile to Impressed.

CX

Identity, security, scoring, certificates, and debrief reports. Iron Clad™ security protects answer keys. Every session generates a performance debrief PDF, a branded certificate, and a shareable Instagram badge.

How It Works

Five Phases. One Decision.

Every simulation follows the same rigorous structure. No shortcuts. No hand-holding. Your decision is final.

Phase 1
BRIEF

Study the case. Run the numbers. Complete the calculation worksheet.

Phase 2
CONSULT

Question AI agents in the boardroom. Extract information. Read the room.

Phase 3
DECIDE

Make your call. Write your reasoning. Lock in your confidence level.

Phase 4
PANEL

Defend your decision under AI cross-examination. No safety net.

Phase 5
SCORED

AI rubric grading. Debrief report. Certificate and badge.
