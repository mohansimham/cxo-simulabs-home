1. The Vision

A multiplayer business simulation platform for all CXO-level roles — not just PM. Product Management is Track 1. Future tracks will cover CMO, CFO, CEO strategy, Sales leadership, and more.

  • A facilitator controls live sessions (Kahoot-style but for serious business training)
  • AI avatars fill empty seats when there aren't enough human players
  • All simulations are grounded in real business case studies using public domain data
  • IP is protected through private GitHub repos + Terms of Use + © notices
  • Track 1 (built): Product Management — 6 simulations live
  • Track 2+: CMO, CFO, CEO Strategy, Sales — to be built

2. Simulation Rebuild Decisions (March 2026)

BrandingFull CXO Simulabs branding throughout. Subtle company-flavored accents (color/icon) per sim — no logos, no trademarked colors, no copyright infringement.
